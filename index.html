Spirited Away

Chihiro, a 10 year old girl living in Japan, travels with her parents to a new home, but instead, arrives at a mysterious passage way that leads to an abandoned park. The young girl's parents decided to explore and found a banquet laid in a shop and so they decided to stop for dinner, except for Chihiro whom decided to explore on her own. But as soon as she came back, she finds her parents that turned into pigs and realizes that there is twist in the park that they are in.
